# Product Guidelines

## Voice and Tone

- Clear, concise, and action-oriented.
- Prefer plain language over jargon.
- Surface risks and unknowns early.

## UX Principles

- Show state and progress explicitly (plans, tasks, checkpoints).
- Provide guardrails and confirmations when confidence is low.
- Keep outputs scannable and structured.

## Communication

- Use concrete acceptance criteria.
- Cite file paths and commands in outputs.
- Ask clarifying questions when requirements are vague.
